# Questions

## Asking hard questions in a work setting

- "Would you mind if I ask you a question that I know my Director is going to ask me?"
- "Can I ask you something that you probably won't want to answer?"
- "Let's pretend I asked you XYZ, what would you say?"
- "I tend to ask a lot of questions, and some of them are kind of direct. Can I have your permission 'up front' to ask them anyway?"
- Be direct — just ask!
