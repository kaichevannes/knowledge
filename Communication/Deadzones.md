If we fumble with passing the conversational ball back, we can get stuck awkwardly
staring at the each other in confused agony, wishing we had something to say.
These are the remedies for those times.

## Senses
Talk about something you can both see, hear, feel: "It's kind of weird for there
to be a TV out here now" (open ended)

## But yeah...
"But yeah, you said before about how..."

## Has anything interesting happened?
"Has anything interesting happened this week?"
"No nothing really stands out, just the same old"
"Well, what about the most interesting? For me, on... (tell a story)"
`Dialogue ensues!`

## Story
"The other day I was..." followed by an open ended question about it.
