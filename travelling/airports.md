# Airports

Arrive early to give plenty of time to get through security.

## Getting through security
- Liquids must be 100ml or under in hand luggage
- Laptop and electronics are placed out of your bag
- Expect having to take shoes off

## Checklist
- [ ] Valid passport (ideally 6+ months)
- [ ] Visa/entry requirements checked (e.g. ESTA for the US)
- [ ] Travel insurance arranged
- [ ] Travel eSIM installed

## On arrival
- Have return flight and accommodation details ready
- Expect long queues
