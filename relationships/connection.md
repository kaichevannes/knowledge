The fundamentals of connection are flirting, paying attention to the other
person and good communication skills.

When we get to know someone, we start with facts and then move to preferences or
opinions -- favourite food, music. When we know someone better, we can add mild
vulnerabilities: personal & moderate positive, or personal & mildly negative.

## Flirting
Flirting is hard to detect on purpose. It is a safe way to communicate interest:
communicating interest with plausible deniability. Humour is an important part
of flirting because you need to be paying attention to the other person,
making someone laugh demonstrates that you understand them.
