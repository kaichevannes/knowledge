# Flirting

Flirting is hard to detect on purpose. It is a safe way to communicate interest:
communicating interest with plausible deniability. Humour is an important part
of flirting because you need to be paying attention to the other person,
making someone laugh demonstrates that you understand them.

## Dealbreakers -- Bad Flirting
1. Slimy approach -- Sexist/slimy comments about looks, inappropriate, rude
2. Bad hygiene
3. Lack of exclusive interest -- "I just want to get laid tonight, there is
  nothing special about you."
4. Different Views
5. Vulgar vocabulary
6. Lack of intelligence
7. Narcissism
8. Lack of humour and low self-esteem
9. Excessive intimacy
10. Poor looks
11. Stinginess
