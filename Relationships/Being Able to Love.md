We grow emotional calluses over time as we get rejected, ghosted, have high
hopes that fall to the wayside. If we don't process these emotions our ability
to form connections weaken.

## Emotional Regulation
1. **Naming your emotions**: activates the linguistic cortex which tones down the
  signal being sent to our amygdala to understand what the signal is saying.
  This is felt as a decrease in volume of emotions we feel, lowering the hurt.
  When we understand what we are feeling, we have more power over the emotion.
2. **Cultivating emotions**: reflecting on the positives in the negatives and
  being cautious when falling in love -- we don't want to fall in love with
  someone that is unhealthy for us.
3. **Not letting emotions control you**: e.g. breaking up when you're angry with
  your partner, getting back together when one month later you're lonely. You
  should feel your emotions, but not let them control your behaviour: "What is
  this anger telling me? What is it motivating me towards?". Life is f***ing
  hard.
