Based on Jungian archetypes. Everyone has within their subconscious a masculine
(Animus) part and a feminine part (Anima). Someone that is half as connected to
their own nurturing component needs their partner to be twice as nurturing. At
the extreme this leads to hyper-masculine men forming relationships with
hyper-feminine women, creating impossible expectations. If men are more in
touch with their feminine side, then the burden of femininity on their partner
becomes less and vice versa.
