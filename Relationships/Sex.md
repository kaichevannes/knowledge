## STIs and pregnancy
Condoms are relatively effective at preventing pregnancy, but still over the
course of a year about 10% of couples using condoms still get pregnant. Though
they are the best option for preventing STIs.

Other options include: IUDs, oral contraceptive pills, etc.

It's important to have a conversation about this.
